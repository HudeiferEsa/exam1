ABOUT THIS SITE

Moodboard

For this site I decided to revisit the pop art theme, this time with the intention of using the bright colours as accents. I was looking for clean and strong contrast, something that was well structured and organised.

Style-Tile

Continuing on the themes built on the moodboard, I decided to keep the bright colours, but to achieve the desires clean lines, I sourced inspiration from the dutch painter Mondrian. I liked how he used primary colours that were similar to my own pallet. I though of maybe wrapping texts in a bright coloured border of some sort. With regards to typography I decided to keep roboto, but for the main texts, I decided to go for Oswald font from google fonts. Oswald creates a balanced contrast against the Roboto.
